⚡ Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard

Driving Data-Driven Insights into Electric Vehicle Adoption and Sustainable Mobility

📖 Project Overview

The Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze the adoption, distribution, and performance of electric vehicles across the United States. The dashboard transforms raw EV registration data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPIs, maps, and trend analysis.

This project demonstrates my ability to perform data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, dashboard design, and business intelligence reporting using Microsoft Power BI.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze the growth of Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption.
Compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV).
Identify the top-performing EV manufacturers and vehicle models.
Analyze electric vehicle registrations across different states.
Monitor CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility.
Provide interactive insights for business decision-making.

📌 Key Business Insights
Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) contribute approximately 77.6% of all registered electric vehicles.
Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) account for approximately 22.4% of total EV registrations.
Electric vehicle registrations have shown significant growth in recent years, indicating rapid adoption.
Several states have emerged as leaders in EV adoption, highlighting regional demand.
CAFV eligibility analysis helps identify vehicles qualified for clean energy incentives.
The dashboard provides a clear comparison of manufacturers and vehicle models to identify market leaders.
